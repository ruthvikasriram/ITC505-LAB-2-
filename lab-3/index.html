Top 5 self-help books for beginners

1. The mountain is you

This book consists of strategies to deal with self-sabotage, how to process your emotions and many more to help you grow.

2. Magic by Rhonda byrne

This book is clearly going to inject gratitude into your life. And gratitude practice literally change your whole life.

3. 48 laws of power

This book conisists of laws on how to behave inorder to be powerful in ones own life.

4. It didn't start with you

This book has all about traumas, generational traumas, how someone's actions can have impact on someone. It makes you realise a lot of things you may not know about you.

5. The courage to be disliked

This book is everyone who is feels insecure and needs approval/attention so that it feels right. It detaches you from other people's opinions and makes you confident.
